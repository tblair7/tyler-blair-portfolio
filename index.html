About Me

As a Senior Data Scientist with experience primarily across gaming and entertainment, I specialize in leveraging data-driven insights to solve complex business challenges and drive measurable impact. With a strong background in data orchestration, machine learning, and data visualization, I've managed end-to-end data pipelines, developed predictive models, and created actionable business intelligence solutions.

I have a proven track record of collaborating with cross-functional teams, from product and engineering to executives, to define data-driven strategies that enhance decision-making and drive business success. My passion for combining technical expertise with business acumen has enabled me to deliver high-impact results, including increasing player retention by 130% through targeted AB testing.

I hold a Master’s in Physical Chemistry from the University of Washington, where I honed my analytical skills. Outside of work, I enjoy running, reading, and spending time with my dog, Benny. I’m always excited to connect with others in the data science community and explore new opportunities to create value through data.
